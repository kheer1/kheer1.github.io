Strategy game for iOS and Android

Fantasy War Tactics is a free to play turn-based strategy role-playing tactical mobile RPG game. There are over 50 different heros that you can customize to your play style. With hours of fun, it's hard to put down.

Things that make FWT a joy to play.

  1. Ton of Heroes
  2. Items
    • Weapons
    • Armor
    • Accessories
  3. Player vs Player
  4. Dungeons
